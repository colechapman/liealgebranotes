# Lie Groups and Lie Algebras — Russian Lecture Notes (Translated)

This repository contains a complete English translation and LaTeX reconstruction of a lecture series on **Lie Groups and Lie Algebras**.

Lie algebras → Universal enveloping algebras → PBW theorem →  
$\mathfrak{sl}_2$ representation theory → Lie groups →  
Exponential map → Integration theorems →  
Nilpotent / solvable algebras → Cartan’s criterion → Semisimple structure theory.

The original material references:

- Vinberg & Onishchik — *Seminar on Lie Groups and Algebraic Groups*
- Serre — *Lie Algebras and Lie Groups*

---

### Lectures 1–3  
- Basic examples of Lie algebras  
- Representations  
- Invariant forms  
- Schur’s lemma  
- Tensor products and duals  
- Irreducible representations of $\mathfrak{sl}_2$

### Lectures 4–5  
- Universal Enveloping Algebra $U(\mathfrak g)$  
- Tensor algebra construction  
- Filtrations and gradings  
- Poincaré–Birkhoff–Witt (PBW) Theorem  

### Lectures 6–7  
- Structure of $\mathfrak{sl}_2$  
- Highest weight theory  
- Verma modules  
- Casimir operator  
- Complete reducibility  
- Characters and tensor product decompositions  

### Lectures 8–10  
- Definition of Lie groups  
- Lie functor  
- Adjoint representation  
- $SU(2)$ and $SO(3)$  
- Universal covers  
- Haar measure  
- Complete reducibility for compact groups  

### Lectures 11–12  
- Exponential map  
- Functoriality  
- Integration of Lie algebra homomorphisms  
- Uniqueness and existence theorems  
- Virtual Lie subgroups  
- Malcev closure  

### Lectures 13–14  
- Nilpotent Lie algebras  
- Solvable Lie algebras  
- Engel’s theorem  
- Lie’s theorem  
- Cartan’s criterion  
- Killing form  
- Semisimple Lie algebras  
- Radical decomposition  

This repository serves as:

- A clean LaTeX reconstruction of a Russian lecture series  
- A structural roadmap into semisimple Lie theory  
- A personal reference for continued study in representation theory
